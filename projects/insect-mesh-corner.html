Insect Mesh Corner

You all know these Insect Mesh Things you can put inside windows to prevent the insects from flying inside.

Someone had some leftover mesh and the side pieces, but no corners left. Instead of ordering a whole new thing, we make the Corners ourselves.

There were 4 pieces milled from Aluminum. The FreeCAD Files are here. Unfortunately there are no pictures of the Part this time, as it is already installed.
